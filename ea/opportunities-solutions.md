## Trade-off Decisions (Explicit)

### Why Not Build Everything at Once?
- High risk
- Low adoption
- Long feedback cycles

### Why Not Start with AI/Automation?
- Low data maturity
- Trust deficit
- High false-positive risk

### Why Incremental Delivery?
- Faster learning
- Lower blast radius
- Stronger enterprise adoption

---

## Execution Readiness Principles

1. Each increment must be deployable independently
2. Backward compatibility is mandatory
3. No increment breaks previous value
4. Human workflows remain in control
5. Adoption maturity drives feature enablement
